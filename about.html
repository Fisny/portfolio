About me

Hi, I’m Fatbardh Fisniku, a -year-old Software & Web Developer born in Albania and raised in Italy.
I arrived in Italy in 2001, settling in Genoa, where I developed a passion for technology and coding.
Since I was a kid, I’ve always been fascinated by technology.
I remember having fun pretending to be a hacker with my friends, using CMD to change the text color from white to green, just like the movies.
That simple curiosity sparked my interest in computing, and from there, my journey into the world of technology began.

Since 2021, I’ve been working as a .NET developer, specializing in technologies such as C#, Azure DevOps, Microsoft SQL, and more.
My experience primarily revolves around the Microsoft tech stack, where I’ve gained hands-on expertise in creating, debugging, and optimizing applications.

I have a particular interest in intranet environments, where I often work with Putty for SSH tunneling to access specific LDAP environments.
Much of my work involves processing files like XML, JSON, and CSV, integrating them into SQL tables, and creating scheduled C# jobs to handle updates and duplicates.
I’ve also worked with Oracle VM VirtualBox to connect virtual machines and monitor data flows.
On the server-side, I’ve configured both test and production environments using IIS Manager, ensuring the smooth deployment of .dll and .exe files.

In addition to backend development, I have a strong background in API creation and testing with Postman, as well as designing efficient database structures, implementing primary and foreign keys, and writing stored procedures and functions to optimize data handling.

I have a passion for enhancing the design and responsiveness of websites. I’ve worked on several projects for DHL, where I focused on creating user-friendly, visually appealing, and responsive sites.

Before transitioning into software development, I spent several years as a non-destructive testing technician, traveling throughout Italy and occasionally across Europe. This experience has helped me develop a strong sense of problem-solving and attention to detail, which I now apply to my development work.
